ZECK TECHNOLOGY:

Is an Entrepreneurship that focuses on Computer literacy programs, WAEC registration for schools, Graphics designing among others things. Zeck Technology was established to promote computer literacy, in other to enhanced the ability of young people in and out of the wall of high school for the technological challenges in our world, with Mr. Nathan D. Richards as its Chief Executive Officer, Mr. Festus G. Frazer, Jr. as Administrator and Mr. Jerry K. Gbaye, Jr. as Director for Training/Literacy at Zeck Technology. Zeck Tech provides its own computers for the school computer literacy program. Since its inception, Zeck Tech as it is called, has worked with many schools and business across Montserrado County and beyond including: J.K Demey Memorial Academy, Ann Sandell Independent School, Little Angel School system, Charles J.S Young AGM among other. It has also work with the Youth Aid Initiatives - Liberia, Youth for Peace and Security Africa, Diversified Youth Network Just to name a few,

OUR VISION STATEMENT:

Zeck Technology, envisions to see a Liberia where young people especailly secondary school students will be computer literate upon leaving the wards of high school there by preparing them to face those real world challenges of information & communication technology.

OUR MISSION STATEMENT:

Promoting computer literacy through affordable and accessable program.

FOUNDING MEMBERS PROFILE:

Mr. Nathan D. Richards was born on May 25, 1993 in Montserrado County, is a young passionate Liberian, an entrepreneur, a class Room Teacher, a Volunteer, ICT officer. He is a graduate of the Nathan E. Gibson Memorial Academy in du-port road, he holds a A+ in Computer Hardware and Software from the NetLib Technical Institute in Congo Town, He also obtain a Certificate in DOP & Graphics from the Icon Computer school in Duala, He went through a six weeks intensive training and obtain a certificate from the Mercy Crop in Job Readiness and Entrepreneurship, he holds a certificate in Microsoft Office Suit from the T-Five Computer in Red Light. Mr. Richards has served in many capacities ranging from class room teacher to IT Officer for many years. He serviced as Computer Instructor for Sinkor AGM High School, Charles J.S Young AGM School, Hardware Instructor at the NetLib in Congo Town, Tough at the AB Computer School on the Peace Island in Congo Town to name a few. He also worked for Governmental institutions and Non-Governmental Institutions (EPA, NEC, Justice Ministry, Youth Aid Initiatives - Liberia, Youth for Peace and Security Africa). He is currently the Chief Executive Officer of Zeck Technology Mr. Richards is a Christian and he worship with the Calvary Church of the Trinity.



Mr. Festus G. Frazer Jr. is a Liberian, a volunteer, a practicing Journalist, a class room teacher.
